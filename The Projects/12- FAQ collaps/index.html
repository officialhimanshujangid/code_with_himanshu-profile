What are the Leadership Principles that Amazon recruiters look for in candidates?

If you're a Software Engineer with 5+ yrs of experience and looking to uplevel your career, preparing for the Leadership Principles will go a long way in your interview prep for Amazon.
Questions around the leadership principles can feature in the on-site behavioral round or the bar-raiser round. A bar-raiser is part of a team of specially trained professionals whose main job is to maintain excellence while hiring candidates for roles across the board at Amazon.

Is web development a dying career?

Web development is certainly not a dying career. Web development is one of the occupations where most expect growth. This profession is not going obsolete anytime soon. Web development is expected to grow to double the percentage of the average of all other occupations. Businesses are nowadays shifting towards increasing their presence on the internet.
